Screen Rules
Candlestick 1D BUY/SELL
Buy when close breaks prior 20D high or bullish engulfing confirms above volume median. Sell when bearish engulfing or long upper wick fails above resistance.
MACD 12/26/9
Buy on fresh bullish cross with rising histogram for 2 sessions. Sell on bearish cross below signal line or histogram deterioration after an extended run.
RSI 14D
Buy when RSI recovers from 45-55 support without entering overbought. Sell when RSI divergence appears above 70 or breaks below 40 with price weakness.
Bollinger Band 20D/2SD
Buy squeeze breakouts that close above upper band with volume. Sell band-walk exhaustion or breakdown below mid-band after failed breakout.
MA Trend 20/50/200
Buy when 20D > 50D > 200D and price holds above 20D. Sell when price loses 50D or 20D crosses below 50D with weak breadth.
Volume Breakout Z-score
Buy when volume is >1.5 sigma above 20D average and price closes strong. Sell when high volume appears on a breakdown candle.
Relative Strength vs SPY
Buy when 20D relative strength makes a higher high while price trend is intact. Sell when relative line breaks 20D support.
ATR / Volatility 14D
Buy controlled breakouts with ATR expansion below risk cap. Sell or avoid when ATR spike implies gap risk or stop distance is too wide.
